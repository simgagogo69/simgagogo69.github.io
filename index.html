How to Play

Use WASD to move.
Press the mouse to fire.
Press U to open the upgrade menu.
Press F to open your inventory and switch weapons.
Press the SPACEBAR to dash ahead in the direction you're moving.
Survive waves if you can, collect weapons and potions, and harness magic.
Watch out for every 10th wave, as powerfull bosses spawn there.
